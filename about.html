About us

Our motive is to make others beautiful, through our essential products. Allowing both men and women to express themselves in the best way possible

Founder's message

I am very passionate about making natural soaps. I learned from my mother at an early age. She taught me how to make homemade soap and body butters from shea butter, natural oils and herbs for home use.

When my daughter Maina was born in 2006, she had a skin condition called Eczema. The doctor prescribed her a cream that I later realized had chemicals in it which are not so helpful. I therefore took matters into my own hands and started making all natural body butters and lotions that I learned from my mother.

Fourteen years later, it is now a successful venture of body butters and soaps for not only my daughter Maina, but my entire family, friends and the world.

All my natural body butters and soaps are made entirely by myself using my decades of experience in making homemade soaps.

For me, making all natural body butters and soaps means avoiding the use of ingredients that could be toxic or manufactured in ways that are not desirable. I do not use artificial dyes, perfumes or additives in my natural soaps. The soaps are made from shea butter, natural oils lye and herbs that are very effective, soothing and healthy.

In addition to selling homemade body butters and soaps, I also have a long standing and passionate interest in hair, hair products, fashion and jewelry. I carry a unique and latest line of hair products, fashion and jewelry.

As a small business owner, I deeply care about providing high quality homemade natural body butter, soaps, hair products, fashion and jewelry to my esteemed clientele. I appreciate your interest and patronage. Please spread the word to your friends and family.
